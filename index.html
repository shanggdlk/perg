About Us

Welcome to the Pittsburgh Experimental Research Group (PERG)! Our group builds cutting-edge technologies in wireless communication, sensing systems, and mobile health applications. Our team is passionate about advancing the integration of wearable devices, health sensors, and wireless technologies for impactful real-world solutions.

Ongoing Research

Computing - Sensor-in-the-loop AI Agent: Developing personalized AI agents through multi-modal sensor technologies. Sensing - Inside-out & Outside-in: Exploring various sensor modalities (acoustic, vision, and many others) for human-centric sensing. Communication - Wireless Digital Twin: Understanding the wireless world through neural ray tracing.
