About Us

Welcome to the Pittsburgh Experimental Research Group (PERG)! We specialize in cutting-edge technologies in wireless communication, sensor networks, and mobile health applications. Our team is passionate about advancing the integration of wearable devices, health sensors, and wireless technologies for impactful real-world solutions.

Ongoing Research

Our research focuses on developing ultra-low-cost health trackers, wireless mobile systems, and sensor technologies that improve healthcare in resource-constrained regions. We are actively working on projects involving wearable health sensors, digital twins for smart cities, and innovative wireless communication protocols.

Wireless Sensing: Exploring advanced sensors for health and environment monitoring. Wireless Communication: Improving connectivity for IoT and mobile health. Mobile Health: Using smartphones for remote health diagnostics and patient monitoring. Wearable Devices: Transforming earbud technologies into health monitoring tools.
